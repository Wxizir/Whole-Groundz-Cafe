About Us

Whole Groundz Cafe is a dream come true I love to drink coffee and every time I drink of coffee at Starbucks or in any coffee house I visualize about having my own coffee shop. Though,it could have remained as a dream till I found the Coffee company Organo Gold (OG) who is offering a coffee shop business for a very affordable price. I availed the package after a year it was introduced to me.I got excited to avail without even having any place yet to start my coffee shop,but with that attractive investment package, I just grabbed the offer right away and said “bahala na” but then I realized it’s not that easy. I have to do something about the products I have on hand or else it'll be all expired and will be gone to waste. After that excitement and realization, I started joining events. I joined the New City Charity Fun Run which is a run for a cause event which of course didn’t really help me gain anything other than getting promoted and the experience I felt when helping people through my business,then joined events like car shows and charity bazaars. After that,I found a small place to rent, small space in a salon. Then 6 months later I found a much bigger place but it’s still a shared place in a salon & spa center. After all those happenings, I finally registered my business name in March 2017 which was Whole Groundz Coffee Hub, which serves as a cafe and of course at the same time I also include coffee distribution of Organo Gold in my business. After a year, of jumping in to this biggest leap of my life, and grab this place while only having only faith & trust in my heart.I changed the hub into cafe, all the hardship plus prayers, trust and big big faith finally paid off, I finally have a Coffee shop that I can call my own and really seeing it under my name. “Whole Groundz Cafe” Whole Groundz Cafe’s Grand Opening was on July 28, 2018. It was one of the most achieving moments of my life.Whole for it's completeness of the business that I am dreaming of. Groundzfor God provided it to be cultivated and be used to sow goodness & reap according to His plans. Whole Groundz Cafe is a coffee shop that serves Gourmet Coffee that provides health benefits in every cup.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our Mission is to create a healthy & friendly place where people connect to each other rather than on Wi-Fi.To serve healthier coffee to people of all ages and be known to be helping people,through improving their health with the benefits it gives in every cup.To provide great, delicious food in a very affordable price.

To create and establish more healthier & friendlier coffee shops,to serve as an encouragement to people that don't have much in their pocket that they can start a business, and finally to show more of God's goodness. Christ Offers Financial (breakthrough) For Everyone, Everywhere (coffee).

Vision

Our vision is to influence others more in the future and help more people with our business with the help of God's love and goodness we will maintain the healthy, friendly and a welcoming place we started.
